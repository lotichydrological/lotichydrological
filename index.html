providing clients with hydro-analytics, water resources engineering, and strategic planning services since 2011

a unique approach

Lotic helps clients improve their use, management and conservation of our valuable water resources. We understand that each project is unique. Our approach involves tailoring our services to match the specific requirements and objectives of our clients, ensuring solutions that are practical, innovative and effective.

hydro-analytics

Whether you're navigating water quality concerns, planning water infrastructure, or assessing environmental impacts, our hydro-analytic services bridge the gap between data and actionable solutions, enabling you to achieve your goals while safeguarding our water resources.

restoration design

Stream ecosystems provide critical habitats for a variety of species. Our restoration design philosophy focuses on leveraging natural processes to create diverse habitats and promote healthier aquatic and riparian communities.

data storytelling

We take raw data and transform it into compelling narratives, visually appealing graphics, charts, and interactive visualizations. These techniques help distill complex information, allowing your audience to grasp insights at a glance.

simulation modeling

We employ state-of-the-art modeling software and custom compute code to devlep hydraulic and hydrological simulations. Our services include rainfall-runoff modeling, flood risk analysis, hydraulic habitat quality evaluations, simulations of bioenergetics, stream temperature predictions, and network analysis.

decision support

We develop decision support tools to empower clients with actionable insights that respond to changing conditions on the ground. These tools help guide informed decisions backed by thorough quantitative data analysis and predictive modeling.

strategic planning

We specialize in developing strategic plans that safeguard water resources for present and future generations. We promote an integrated approach that considers all aspects of water management. Our plans include thorough risk assessments that identify vulnerabilities and enable proactive risk mitigation.
